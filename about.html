About Central Zoo

Connecting People with Wildlife Since 1980

Our Mission

Central Zoo is a well-established wildlife park that offers visitors a unique opportunity to explore and experience nature closely. It is home to a wide variety of animals, birds, and reptiles, providing both entertainment and educational value.

Our History

The zoo was originally established in 1980 with the aim of protecting wildlife and creating awareness. Over the years, it has grown significantly and gained popularity among visitors worldwide.

Growth & Expansion

In the year 2000, the zoo was expanded to cover 33 acres. This allowed for more space and improved facilities, creating a natural and comfortable environment for our animals.

Our Commitment

We are committed to the care and protection of animals. Proper feeding, medical care, and safe habitats are our top priorities. We play a vital role in educating the public about conservation.

Experience the wildlife like never before at Central Zoo. A place of conservation and education.
